UI Engineer Income

The estimated total pay for a UI Engineer is 124,271 dollars per year in the United States. With an average total salary of 109,577 dollars per year, UI engineers get paid pretty well.
A UI engineer gets a salary that is similar to that of an Airline Captain.
The top 10% of UI Engineers make 126k or more a year. The bottom 10% of UI Engineers make 88k a year.
